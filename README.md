## Joining The Server (With Pictures!)
### Step 0
- Ensure you have Escape From Tarkov installed **AND UPDATED** on your computer.
  <img width="699" height="112" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/85113ac8-dab9-453a-a1c6-1689c41acda2" />
### Step 1
- Create a folder in your C:\ drive and name it `SPT`</br>
  <img width="225" height="34" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c67899cf-b7b7-4d42-92c8-2c0e499b1851" /></br>
  <img width="597" height="22" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64c93956-993d-40d8-80a5-666a8026ff69" />
### Step 2
- Get the first download: [SPT Installer](https://forge.sp-tarkov.com/installer)
- Move the installer into the folder you just created</br>
  <img width="610" height="139" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1c45bfe7-b86a-49ef-b71f-c06db2dd8170" /></br>
- Run the installer.
- IF you do not have the dependencies (.Net Runtimes) you will see red 'X's, you can click these to be taken to their respective download pages. After downloading, close and relaunch the installer.
  <img width="815" height="444" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6066e1df-6601-4ba9-b411-5dc34909e078" />
</br></br>
- Run `SPT.Server` once until you see this message, then close it.</br>
  <img width="308" height="32" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8fd5b761-ebbf-4bb1-a22e-d93ca6a0afb4" />


### Step 3
- Get the second download: [Project Fika](https://github.com/project-fika/Fika-Installer/releases/tag/v1.1.7)

- Move the installer into `SPT` and launch it</br>
  <img width="684" height="563" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/340ba19a-1bcb-46b4-ada5-b43dc3e3fc75" /></br>
- Follow the prompts until done.

### Step 4
- Get.. all the other downloads. Specifically all of them with the `Mod` Description. For now, just download them all and extract them straight into your downloads folder. </br>
  <img width="699" height="277" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0e2d4ddd-46db-4ceb-a05b-c104ef9ef1a1" /></br>

- After you've extracted everything create a temporary folder so that we can combine them all.</br>
- Move the _**CONTENTS**_ of all the mod folders into the temp folder</br>
  <img width="1999" height="775" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d055a205-8d25-4b4b-ab1d-03e114f24d77" /></br>
- After this you should have a downloads folder full of empty folders, and a temp folder with 2 folders of a ton of combined files.

### Step 5
- Move the two folders of combined files directly into the SPT folder. </br>
  <img width="2002" height="773" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ae65fc57-1398-43ac-8640-4ceb4573ee48" /></br>

### Step 6 (Almost there)
- Run `SPT.Launcher` from your `SPT` folder. </br>
- Click `Settings` when it inevitably fails.
  <img width="773" height="486" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0b304e82-a87a-4e48-b9ac-6dbe31e48de3" /></br>
- Enable the `Developer Mode` check box and replace the default URL with `https://10.50.0.1:6969`</br></br>
  <img width="371" height="63" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/46b1940c-443e-4d2c-b538-b0b9fb8778c7" />

### Step 7
- Create your username and Click `Login/Register`</br>
- Choose `SPT Zero to hero` when prompted to create a new one. Please note that other account types have in the past caused issues and **_have been lost forever_**. Use at your own risk.</br>
  <img width="508" height="392" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9f2df644-47ff-4215-9f7c-a38b58597507" /></br></br>
